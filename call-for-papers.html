How to Submit

Submission Guidelines

01

Prepare Your Abstract

Abstracts should be 250-300 words, clearly stating objectives, methodology, findings, and significance. Include 3-5 keywords.

02

Format Your Paper

Full papers (optional) should be 4-8 pages in IEEE or Springer format. Use the provided template for consistency.

03

Submit Online

Upload your submission through our online portal. You'll receive confirmation and tracking updates.

04

Peer Review

All submissions undergo double-blind peer review by our international program committee.
