FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit an abstract?

Abstracts can be submitted through our online submission portal. Visit the Call for Papers page for detailed guidelines and submission link.

What is the registration deadline?

Early bird registration closes on February 15, 2026. Regular registration is open until March 30, 2026. On-site registration will be available with additional charges.

Is there accommodation available?

Yes, we have partnered with several hotels near the venue offering special conference rates. Visit our Venue & Travel page for accommodation options.

Can I present virtually?

Yes, we offer virtual presentation options for participants unable to travel. Please select "Virtual Presentation" during registration.

Will I receive a certificate?

All registered participants will receive a certificate of attendance. Presenters will receive a separate certificate for their presentation.

What is the dress code?

Business casual attire is recommended for the conference. Formal attire is suggested for the gala dinner and inaugural ceremony.
